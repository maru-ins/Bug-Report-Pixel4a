“Battery replacement recommended” Battery capacity and charging performance are reduced, and battery replacement is recommended. Visit g.colpixel/4abattery for options, including free battery replacement (if eligible)."

| I tried to do a little analysis regarding the Battery replacement compensation program. Which users who get bugs must have :

  1. Reporting Link (g.colpixel/4abattery)
  2. User has original Imei & E-mail Account registered on the Google Pixel 4a 4g Device.
  3. And enter personal data, such as (Real Name, Mobile Number, Region, Full Address, Email Address, Bank Account and other details).
  4. Then already and or create a payoneer account to receive payments (after going through the verification process of the registered bank).

After following all the processes mentioned and provided, and the disbursement process for approximately (21 days). And I managed to receive the compensation money, I thank you very much, regarding my battery which became very very wasteful from before the update.
